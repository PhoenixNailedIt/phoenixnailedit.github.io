FAQs & Rules

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a cancellation fee. Please reach out to us via email or phone to cancel.

Do you offer walk-ins?

We currently only accept appointments made in advance through our booking system. Please use the "Book Now" button to schedule your appointment.

What should I do before my appointment?

Please arrive with clean, dry nails. If you're coming for a manicure or acrylics, make sure your nails are free of old polish and in good condition.

What is your policy on late arrivals?

If you arrive late, we may need to shorten the length of your service to accommodate other appointments. Please try to arrive on time for your appointment!

Can I bring my own nail designs?

Absolutely! Feel free to bring inspiration pictures or your own nail design ideas. We love creating custom nail art!
